## Thai Red Curry with Chicken

One of my favorite weeknight dinners. Takes about 30 minutes.

### Ingredients

- 2 chicken breasts, cubed
- 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
- 2 tbsp red curry paste (or make from scratch — see notes)
- 1 stalk fresh lemongrass, bruised
- 4 kaffir lime leaves
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 200g green beans, trimmed
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp palm sugar (or brown sugar)
- Fresh Thai basil leaves
- Jasmine rice for serving

### Method

1. Heat a splash of coconut cream (the thick part) in a wok over high heat
2. Add curry paste and fry until fragrant, about 1 minute
3. Add chicken and sear on all sides
4. Pour in remaining coconut milk, add lemongrass and lime leaves
5. Simmer 10 minutes, then add vegetables
6. Cook another 5 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp
7. Season with fish sauce and sugar. Taste and adjust
8. Serve over jasmine rice with fresh basil scattered on top

### Notes

- The fresh lemongrass makes a HUGE difference vs. paste
- For extra heat, add sliced bird's eye chilies
- Leftovers reheat well but add a splash of coconut milk
- Works great with shrimp instead of chicken (add shrimp later, cook 3 min only)
