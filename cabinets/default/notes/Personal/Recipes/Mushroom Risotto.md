## Mushroom Risotto

My go-to dinner party dish. Rich, creamy, and always impresses.

### Ingredients

- 300g arborio rice
- 400g mixed mushrooms (porcini, shiitake, button)
- 1L warm vegetable stock
- 1 onion, finely diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 150ml dry white wine
- 60g parmesan, freshly grated
- 30g butter
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Fresh thyme and parsley
- Truffle oil (optional, for finishing)

### Method

1. Sauté mushrooms in olive oil until golden. Season and set aside
2. In the same pan, sweat onion in butter until soft (5 min)
3. Add garlic and rice, stir 1-2 minutes until rice is translucent
4. Deglaze with wine, stir until absorbed
5. Add stock one ladle at a time, stirring and waiting for absorption
6. After 18-20 minutes, rice should be al dente and creamy
7. Fold in mushrooms, parmesan, and a knob of butter
8. Rest 2 minutes covered, then serve with parsley and optional truffle oil

### Notes

- Use dried porcini soaked in warm water for extra umami (add soaking liquid to stock)
- Do NOT rush the stock additions — patience is key
- Good parmesan is non-negotiable
