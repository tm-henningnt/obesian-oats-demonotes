## Iceland Ring Road — July 2026 🇮🇸

### Overview

10-day road trip around Iceland's Ring Road (Route 1). Traveling with Marte. Flights and car rental booked!

### Logistics

- **Flights:** Oslo → Keflavík, July 10-20
- **Car:** Toyota RAV4 4WD from Blue Car Rental
- **Budget:** ~NOK 40,000 total (flights, car, accommodation, food, activities)
- **Accommodation:** Mix of guesthouses, Airbnb, and 2 nights camping
