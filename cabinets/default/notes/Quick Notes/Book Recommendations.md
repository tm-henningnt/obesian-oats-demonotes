## Book Recommendations from Friends & Colleagues

### From Erik

- **"Designing Data-Intensive Applications"** by Martin Kleppmann — "The database book. Every backend dev should read it." Already on my [[Personal/Reading List|reading list]].
- **"Algorithms to Live By"** — "Makes you see algorithms everywhere in daily life"

### From Sofie

- **"Refactoring UI"** by Adam Wathan & Steve Schoger — "Changed how I think about design as a developer"
- **"Don't Make Me Think"** by Steve Krug — "UX classic, short and practical"

### From Maja

- **"Data Mesh"** by Zhamak Dehghani — "Revolutionary for data platform thinking"
- **"Streaming Systems"** by Tyler Akidau — "Dense but worth it for real-time data"

### From the TLDR Newsletter

- **"Build"** by Tony Fadell — "Product lessons from the guy who made the iPod"
- **"An Elegant Puzzle"** by Will Larson — "Engineering management done right"

### Podcasts Recommended

- **Syntax.fm** — Web dev, recommended by Sofie
- **Go Time** — Go language, recommended by Erik
- **Staff Eng Podcast** — Engineering leadership, found this myself
