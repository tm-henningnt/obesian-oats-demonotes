## Team Offsite — Spring 2026

### When?

Late April or early May, after the major migration milestones are done. 2 days + 1 evening. Avoid Easter week.

### Where?

Options:
1. **Holmenkollen** — Scandic hotel, good meeting rooms, city views. ~NOK 2,500/person
2. **Drøbak** — Reenskaug Hotel, charming coastal town, good seafood. ~NOK 2,000/person
3. **Hadeland Glassverk** — Creative vibes, glass-blowing workshop option. ~NOK 2,800/person

### Agenda Ideas

**Day 1 — Strategy**
- Q2/Q3 roadmap deep dive
- Architecture vision: where are we in 2 years?
- Innovation time: each person pitches one "crazy idea" for 5 minutes
- Customer journey mapping exercise

**Day 1 — Evening**
- Team dinner (company pays)
- Activity: bowling, escape room, or wine tasting?

**Day 2 — Team Building + Tactical**
- Retrospective on H1 (bigger than sprint retros)
- Tech debt prioritization (voting exercise)
- Learning goals — each person picks one skill to develop in Q3
- Lightning talks: everyone presents something non-work for 5 min
